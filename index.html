Self Image!

Self image is how someone sees or portrays oneself. it comes in 3 forms:

  • Resulting from how YOU see YOURSELF
  • Resulting how OTHERS see YOU
    • Resulting how YOU perceive how OTHERS see you

In order to live a healthy, happy life, your self image should be positive. Anegative self image can lead to destuctive descisions. there are many ways you can promote the self image of yourself and those around you.

Destroy Image Myth!

A large contributor to negative self image is "Image Myth". These are values that society deems "Beautiful" and expects people to live up to these values. In all actuality, these stardards are unrealistic and in some cases unhealthy.
