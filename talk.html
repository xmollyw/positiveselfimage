How to talk to others about self image

Talking to Friends About Your Own Self Image:

When talking to friends about your self image, they should respond positively. Friends should not pressure you into doing anything wrong or dangerous. True friendship is seeing the beauty in eachother. If a friend puts you down in any way or pressures you into doing something you don't want to do, they aren't good to be around! The truth is, you are beautiful, no matter what body type.

Talking to Friends About Their Self Image:

Talking about others' self esteem can be difficult. If a friend chooses to open up to you about their low self-esteem, let them know that they are loved and keep in mind that it took a lot of courage for them to confide in you. Even if they don't have low self-esteem, it is important to let people know that you care about them. If everyone is nice to each other, it makes happier world without low self-esteem.
